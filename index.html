About Rajinikanth

Shivaji Rao Gaekwad (born 12 December 1950), RAJNIKANTH our beloved only Superstar THALAIVAüî•üî•, is an Indian actor, producer, and screenwriter who works primarily in Tamil cinema. Referred to as "Thalaivar" (leader) and "Superstar" by his fans, he has been described as one of the greatest actors in the history of Indian cinema.

His distinctive acting style, including his mannerisms, dialogue delivery, and unique charisma, has earned him a massive fan following globally. Starting his career with antagonistic and supporting roles, he quickly rose to prominence as a leading man, establishing himself as a commercial powerhouse.

Achievements and Awards

  • Padma Vibhushan (2016) - India's second-highest civilian honor.
  • Padma Bhushan (2000) - India's third-highest civilian honor.
  • Dadasaheb Phalke Award (2019) - India's highest honor in cinema.
  • Recipient of numerous Filmfare Awards South, Cinema Express Awards, and Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.
  • Honored with the Chevalier de l'Ordre des Arts et des Lettres by the Government of France in 2016.
  • One of the highest-grossing actors in the history of Indian cinema.
